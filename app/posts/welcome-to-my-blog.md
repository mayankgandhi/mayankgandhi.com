# Welcome to My Blog

I'm excited to launch this blog where I'll be sharing my thoughts, experiences, and learnings from my journey as an iOS engineer. With over 6 years of experience building native applications, I've had the opportunity to work on some fascinating projects and learn from incredibly talented teams.

## What You Can Expect

This blog will cover a variety of topics, including:

- **iOS Development**: Deep dives into Swift, SwiftUI, UIKit, and modern iOS architectures
- **Software Engineering**: Best practices, design patterns, and lessons learned
- **App Development**: Behind-the-scenes looks at building my personal projects like Cashew and Walnut
- **Career Growth**: Tips on leveling up as an engineer and navigating the tech industry

## A Bit About Me

I'm currently working as a Senior iOS Engineer, where I've had the privilege of:

- Architecting multi-app ecosystems using modern tools like Tuist
- Optimizing app performance through systematic profiling
- Building features that have reached millions of users
- Mentoring developers and establishing best practices

I'm passionate about creating delightful user experiences and writing clean, maintainable code.
